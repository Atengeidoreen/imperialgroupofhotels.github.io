Our Gallery

gallery

A must-see for art lovers, featuring a diverse collection of works by both established and emerging artists. The gallery showcases paintings, sculptures, and other art forms from Uganda and beyond. Guests can also enjoy a drink at the gallery's bar, which offers a relaxed atmosphere to take in the artwork. Guided tours of the gallery are available, providing visitors with insight into the artists and their creations.
